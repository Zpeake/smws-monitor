Privacy Policy
This SMS program is operated by Zac Peake.
Your privacy is important to me. Here’s how your information is handled:
Information Collected: Only your phone number is collected for the purpose of sending personal messages, reminders, or notifications.
Use of Information: Your phone number will only be used to send messages you have requested or agreed to receive. No unsolicited messages will be sent.
Sharing of Information: Your phone number and any personal information will not be shared, sold, or disclosed to any third parties.
Opt-Out: You can opt out of receiving messages at any time by replying “STOP.” Once you opt out, your phone number will no longer be used for messaging.
Data Security: Reasonable measures are taken to protect your information from unauthorized access or disclosure.
If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please contact me personally, as you can't have access if you don't know me.
